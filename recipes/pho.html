Pho

Description

The Pho soup broth is everything. Itâ€™s light yet full of flavour, deceptively beefy, savoury, complex, has the tiniest hint of richness and is filled with beautiful spices like cinnamon.

It is, without question, one of The Best Soups in the whole world!

Ingredients

  • Brisket
  • Meaty bones
  • Marrow bones
  • Rice noodles
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Sriracha
  • Fish sauce
  • Ginger
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Quick boil
  2. Scum
  3. Wash
  4. Simmer for 3 hours
  5. Remove brisket
  6. Simmer 40 minutes further
  7. Strain
  8. Ladle into bowls over noodles
